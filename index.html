How It Works

This challenge follows a simple progressive overload principle:

  • Day 1 of the year: Do 1 push-up
  • Day 2 of the year: Do 2 push-ups
  • Day 100 of the year: Do 100 push-ups
  • And so on...

The chart above shows your daily progress, with darker colors representing more push-ups for that day.

By following this challenge, you'll gradually build strength and endurance throughout the year.
